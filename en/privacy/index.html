Privacy Policy

This page explains the basic privacy principles for the Pelicano website and the Pelicano application.

1. Local-first behavior

Pelicano is a local-first Windows app. By default, clipboard history and related settings are stored on your device, and clipboard contents are not uploaded to an operator-managed server as a normal product behavior.

2. Information that may be processed by the website

This website is a static page. Normal hosting-level access logs and download request metadata may be processed by the hosting infrastructure when pages or installer files are served.

3. App network behavior

The app may contact a remote manifest endpoint to check for updates or version information. At this time, it is not intended to rely on user accounts, ad tracking, or marketing analytics.

4. Your control

Local clipboard data and settings are primarily controlled by you on your own device. If your workflow involves sensitive information, you should decide whether and how to use the app based on your own security requirements.

5. Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent through GitHub Issues.

6. Effective date

Effective date: March 15, 2026
