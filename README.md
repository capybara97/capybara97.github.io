# Pelicano

Pelicano is a smart clipboard manager for Windows that helps you save, search, and reuse everything you copy.

Instead of losing important text every time you copy something new, Pelicano keeps your clipboard history organized so you can bring back the right item whenever you need it. It is designed for people who work fast and copy a lot, including students, developers, researchers, marketers, and office workers.

## What Pelicano Does

- Stores your copied text in clipboard history automatically
- Lets you open a quick selection popup with `Ctrl + Shift + V`
- Helps you find old snippets instantly with keyword search
- Reduces repetitive copy-and-paste work
- Prevents accidental loss of important copied text
